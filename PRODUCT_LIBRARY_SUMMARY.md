### ESG System Integration
Each green product has characteristics that the AI-powered ESG system can identify:
- **Low carbon materials:** GGBS, PFA, reclaimed bricks, lime mortar
- **Recycled content:** Aggregates, steel, wall ties
- **Renewable/natural:** Timber, wood fibre insulation, natural oils
- **Low VOC:** Water-based coatings and treatments
- **Certified sustainable:** FSC/PEFC timber

### Realistic Specifications
All products represent realistic choices that architects face:
- **Different price points:** Economy, standard, premium
- **Performance trade-offs:** Thermal vs. fire performance, strength vs. weight
- **Availability considerations:** Standard vs. specialist products
- **Technical requirements:** Certifications, standards compliance
- **Regional variations:** UK-specific manufacturers and standards
